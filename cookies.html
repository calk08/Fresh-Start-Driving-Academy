Cookies Policy

This website uses cookies to improve your experience and to help us understand how visitors use the site. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionalities.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies – Required for basic site functionality, such as remembering your cookie preferences and ensuring the website works properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies – Help us measure how visitors use the website, which pages are most popular, and how we can improve our service.
  • Marketing cookies – Used to show relevant content and enable embedded services like Google Maps. These help us provide interactive features and location services.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your cookie consent preferences
  • To provide essential website functionality
  • To enable Google Maps integration for our location services
  • To analyze website traffic and improve our services
  • To ensure our website performs optimally across different devices

Your Choices

You have full control over which cookies you accept:

  • Accept all cookies – Enables all website features including maps, analytics, and enhanced functionality
  • Reject non-essential cookies – Only essential cookies will be used, which may limit some website features
  • Reset preferences – You can change your mind at any time using the button below
